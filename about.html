About.

Welcome! We're all about making cup holders that are both well-designed, work great and are good for the planet.

Our team is really into changing how drinks are carried and enjoyed. We've got everything from simple single-cup holders to systems that can haul multiple drinks. Our cup holder boxes and carriers are made to make life easier for stores, customers, and the earth.

🌿 How We Got Started

We noticed something simple: it's harder than it should be to carry a bunch of drinks without spilling. Cafés, restaurants, and delivery places were dealing with spills, flimsy packaging, and materials that weren't good for the environment.
We thought we could do better—make tougher, more practical, and eco-friendly cup holders.

We started small, messing around with molded pulp designs in a workshop. Now, we're a packaging company that helps all sorts of businesses. These days, we create, design, and sell cool cup holder options to businesses all over the world, from local coffee shops to huge drink companies.

We've spent years learning, listening to customers, and trying out new materials. Because of that, we’ve created a line of cup holders that are strong, comfy to carry, and easy on the planet.

⚙️ What We're About

We design and make top-notch cup holders and drink carriers for lots of different uses.

Here's what we offer:

Single-cup holders: Great for one drink to go.

Two and four-cup carriers: Super popular with coffee shops, fast-food places, and caterers.

Custom-printed cup holder boxes: Add your brand and get the word out.

Eco-friendly molded pulp carriers: Made from stuff that can be recycled, and breaks down naturally.

Plastic and reusable cup holders: Perfect for cars, desks, or using again and again.

Car cup holder boxes and organizers: Make transport easier and safer.

Cool combo carriers: Using paper, cardboard, and insulation to make them stronger.

Every cup holder is carefully made to be stable, prevent spills, and be easy to hold. Our designs fit all sorts of cups, from tiny espresso cups to big tumblers, so they work with almost any drink business.

🏗️ How We Do It

Each cup holder goes through a careful process of design, testing, and making sure it's top quality:

Research &amp; Design: Our team looks at drink trends, cup sizes, and how people carry things to come up with practical designs.

Picking Materials: We use good stuff like recycled paper pulp, cardboard, bamboo fiber, and materials that break down naturally.

Making &amp; Trying Out Prototypes: We test each cup holder to see how strong and stable it is, and how comfy it is to carry. This way, we know it'll work in the real world.

Checking for Sustainability: We make sure every step uses as little waste and energy as possible.

Making Lots &amp; Making Sure They're Good: We use machines and check everything carefully to make sure every cup holder is the same and works well.

Getting Them Out There: We sell to customers near and far, and we can handle big orders.

This way, we know every cup holder is useful, strong, and good for the environment.
